ABOUT 👤

Hi!😊 I am a dual Ph.D. student at Nanjing University and The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, where I am co-supervised by Prof. Yuan Du from NJU and Prof. Dan Wang from PolyU. I'm also an intern at the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence, BAAI, under the supervision of Prof. Shanghang Zhang from Peking University. My broad interests lie in Efficient and Generalization Learning towards Open-world Multimodal Systems, especially for applications of autonomous driving and robots.

Previously, I received the M.Phil. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, where I am supervised by Prof. Fangxin Wang, and received the B.Eng. and B.M. degree from the joint program of Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications and Queen Mary University of London.
