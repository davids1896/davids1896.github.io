ABOUT 👤

Hi!😊 I am a Master student at Peking University, where I am supervised by Prof. Shanghang Zhang I'm also an intern at the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence, BAAI. Previously, I received the B.Eng degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I won the national championship in the Robomaster National Competition of the National College Student Robotics Competition under the supervision of Engineer Yifei Qin, on behalf of Team Jiao Long. We have many detailed and practical tutorials/open-source code for the Robomaster competition.

My broad interests lie in embodied AI, particularly in robot imitation learning, with hands-on experience in the development and use of various humanoid, wheeled, single-arm, and dual-arm robots.
